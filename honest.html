Honest Auto Care

Honest Auto Care was added to the family in the year 2006 by Mr.Mohammad Toufiq Sayed. Strategically located on the main road in Ras Al Khor, opposite Al Futtaim Carillion before Auto Market Signal, Mr.Mohammad Toufiq Sayed was driven by his ambitions to start up a specialized Auto Garage which 2 years later after working multiple day and night shifts introduced a very challenging competition line for accidental and auto repairs in Dubai’s service industry. He is into this mechanical field since 30 years which includes the experience of AL FUTTAIM MOTORS for 25 years in UAE.
