About Us

Honesty & Perfection Cleaning Services has grown as an established leader providing range of service in cleaning environmental Care and security systems having Head Office in Dubai. We are in this business for about nine years. We have specialized ourselves in every aspects of cleaning chemicals and the equipments involved

Vision

At Honesty and Perfection customer satisfaction is our prime objective. We seek to distinguish ourselves by providing the most possible flexibility and reliability in Customer service. All our staff are well trained and continuous training is imparted to them in their respective fields of exposure to meet growing needs of customers and international standards.

Mission

Honesty and perfection group came to into existence in the year 2002 as a result of the demand that existed locally in automotive industry for outsourcing their non-core business activities like, vehicle washing, cleaning, rust proofing, Body polish, interior cleaning, need of outsourced labor staff like drivers, cleaners etc.
